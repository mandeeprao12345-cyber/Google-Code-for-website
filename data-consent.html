Data Upload Consent

Last updated: 14 June 2026

By uploading a document to InstantTaxFile.com, you consent to the processing of that document for tax-related assistance, AI-assisted analysis, professional review, and service delivery.

Documents You May Upload

You may upload tax notices, Form 16, investment proofs, income statements, broker statements, screenshots, PDFs, or other tax-related documents required for the selected service.

Purpose of Processing

Your document may be used to generate an AI-assisted summary, identify key tax details, support tax filing, help our CA or professional team review your case, communicate with you, and maintain service records.

Third-Party Processing

Your uploaded document may be processed using third-party AI, storage, communication, email, payment, or workflow providers. Do not upload documents unless you agree to this processing.

Sensitive Information

Tax documents may contain PAN, Aadhaar references, income details, TDS, employer details, bank details, investment details, property details, and identity information. Please upload only documents needed for the service.

AI Limitations

AI analysis may be incorrect, incomplete, or unsuitable for your specific case. Do not rely only on AI output for filing, legal, tax, financial, or notice-response decisions. Professional review is recommended.

Storage and Retention

Uploaded documents may be stored for service delivery, professional review, record-keeping, legal compliance, dispute handling, and support. You may request deletion, subject to legal, tax, accounting, and service record requirements.

Withdrawal of Consent

You may request withdrawal of consent or deletion of uploaded documents by contacting us. Withdrawal may affect our ability to provide services already requested.
