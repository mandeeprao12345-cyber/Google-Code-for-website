Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

InstantTaxFile.com provides income tax filing, consultation, document analysis, notice response, and related support services in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, service selected, payment details, payment ID, tax documents, income details, Form 16, tax notices, investment proofs, chat questions, and other information you submit to us.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide tax filing services, verify payments, contact you, analyze documents, respond to queries, prepare tax-related work, improve our services, maintain records, and comply with legal or regulatory requirements.

Document Uploads and AI Analysis

If you upload documents, they may be processed using third-party AI tools to generate summaries or tax-related insights. AI output may be inaccurate and must be reviewed by our team or a qualified professional before relying on it.

Payments

Payments are processed through Razorpay. We do not store your card, UPI, net banking, or wallet credentials on our servers. We may store payment ID, order ID, service selected, and payment status for service and accounting records.

Sharing of Information

We may share information with Chartered Accountants, tax professionals, payment processors, email providers, cloud storage providers, AI service providers, and legal or regulatory authorities where required for providing services or complying with law.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, tax records, dispute handling, and legitimate business purposes. You may request deletion, subject to legal or compliance requirements.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or withdrawal of consent for your personal data, subject to applicable law and service/legal requirements.

Children's Data

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without lawful guardian consent.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.
